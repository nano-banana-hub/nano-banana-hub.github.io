# BananaHub Community Template Policy

## Purpose

BananaHub publishes a mixed ecosystem:

- code and site infrastructure
- official BananaHub-authored templates
- community-contributed template repositories

This policy covers how community template contributions are accepted, attributed, indexed, and, when necessary, removed.

## Recommended license model

- code: MIT or Apache-2.0
- prompt/workflow template content: `CC-BY-4.0`
- pure public-domain template content: `CC0-1.0`

If a template repo mixes code and content, license them separately and make the boundary explicit.

## Submission requirements

Anyone submitting a template for curation, discovery, or direct ingestion into BananaHub represents that:

1. they have the right to publish the template and any included samples
2. the template metadata is truthful, especially `author`, `source`, and `license`
3. they are not uploading material that requires a more restrictive license than the one they declare

Published templates should include:

- frontmatter `license` using a recognizable SPDX or Creative Commons identifier
- a repo `LICENSE` file that matches the declared template license
- accurate attribution metadata
- samples that the submitter can redistribute

## What BananaHub may do with submitted templates

By submitting or requesting indexing of a template, the submitter grants BananaHub a non-exclusive right to:

- fetch and cache template metadata
- index the template in machine-readable catalogs
- display the template title, description, author, tags, declared license, and install command
- generate thumbnails or preview crops from submitted sample images when needed for the catalog

The submitter keeps copyright. BananaHub does not claim exclusive ownership of community templates.

## Third-party material

Do not submit templates or samples that contain:

- copyrighted images, logos, or brand assets without permission
- private datasets or confidential material
- text copied from third-party paid courses, books, or prompt packs without redistribution rights

## Moderation and takedown

BananaHub may remove or hide templates that:

- declare no usable license
- contain infringing or deceptive material
- impersonate authorship or source
- create legal or safety risk for the catalog

Discovered status is not endorsement. Curated status is discretionary.
